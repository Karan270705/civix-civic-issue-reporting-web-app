How it works

From complaint to resolution — in three steps

No bureaucracy. No waiting. Just citizens working together to fix what's broken.

01

Report a problem

Spot a broken road, garbage dump, or faulty streetlight? Post it with a photo, location, and description. Takes 30 seconds.

02

Community validates

Neighbors upvote real issues and downvote spam. When enough people confirm it, it gets marked as verified.

03

Solutions are tracked

Anyone can propose a solution. The best ones rise to the top. Track progress from reported to resolved.
