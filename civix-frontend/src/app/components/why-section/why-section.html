Why it matters

Your voice can lead to real change

Most civic problems stay unfixed — not because nobody cares, but because there's no structured way to report, validate, and track them.

Civix creates accountability. When a community collectively validates a problem and proposes solutions, it becomes impossible to ignore.

Transparency

Every issue is public. Everyone can see what's reported and what's being done.

Community-driven

No single authority decides what matters. The community validates together.

Trackable progress

Follow every issue from reported → verified → in progress → resolved.
